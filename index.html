Animal Geolocation and Smart Monitoring with Notifications

Track the location of animals and monitor their wellbeing across extensive farming conditions in near real-time and receive notifications when animals move beyond set boundaries or exhibit irregular movement or behaviour.

agriSmartTag to protect your livestock

agriSmartTag uses the latest technology to allow farmers to track and monitor their livestock across wide-ranging farming conditions and reduce preventable losses, ease farming operations and improve animal health and profitability.

It gives farmers the ability to set geofence boundaries for animals and receive notification if an animal moves beyond the geofence.
Data intelligence analyses the animal location and movement data and contextual metrics to identify possible health concerns and instantly notifies the farmer so that the farmer can investigate and intervene to address any health and welfare concerns.

Alerts

Be alerted when an animal is static for a long time, have reduced mobility and movement, breaches a boundary fence or is stolen.
Farmers can set and receive alerts based on their own preference and management approach. Our products allow you to define various alerts based on animal movement, location and deviation from normal or expected behaviour.

Manage geofences

Create a single or series of geofences based on predefined paddock or property boundaries. Manage geofences by adding or blocking animals from the area and review grazing patterns from the historical grazing data.

Create animal groups and sub-groups and assign them to specific geofences.
Set dynamic geofence positions such as feeder or water troughs, define specific rules for animals to visit these positions during a specific timeframe and receive alerts if these requirements are not met.
